Complete Care for Everyday Whitening

WhiteScience Multi-Action Whitening Toothpaste is a unique formulation that removes stains and whitens teeth in two ways: a mild abrasive breaks the surface tension of stains so a natural whitening agent can lift them away from tooth enamel. Its alcohol-free freshmint formula cleans with comfort, leaving a pleasing, cooling sensation that keeps your mouth feeling clean and your breath fresh.

A dynamic combination of ingredients formulated to provide total care:

  • Stain Releasing Action: papaya extract, a natural enzyme, breaks down film on your teeth so stains can be released and cleansed away.
  • Cleansing: dicalcium phosphate works as a mild abrasive, like tiny scrubbers, to remove stains released by the papaya extract.
  • Whitening: dicalcium phosphate, properly combined with hydrated silica, helps remove both stains and plaque.
  • Enamel Strengthening: sorbitol, kaolin and fluoride work together to reduce decay, remineralise weakened enamel and improve the toughness and opacity of tooth enamel.
